What is “AI Slop” (and How to Avoid It)?

“AI Slop” is generic, robotic, or spammy output from tools like ChatGPT—unpersonalized, uninspired, and easy for prospects to spot (and delete). This guide highlights what to look out for—and how to use AI to support your unique expertise.

“AI Slop” = Anything that feels like it was copy-pasted from a robot. AI is most helpful when you use it as a starting point, then let your experience and relationships come through.

Rise Above the Noise: Human-First AI

AI can multiply your reach and efficiency, but only you can deliver the human relationships that AMP is known for. The best results come when AI is a tool for amplifying your unique expertise.

DEFINING YOUR PERSONAL BRAND VOICE

Why it matters:
Your personal brand voice is how clients experience you—not just your station or your product. It’s the “vibe” that sets you apart from every other salesperson they hear from. In a world of bland, automated messages, a consistent, authentic voice is what gets you noticed and builds trust.

How to Define It (The Simple Version):

  • Your top sales strengths
  • Your core values
  • Your style for building relationships
  • What you want clients to say about you
  • The feeling you want to leave people with
✔️ Copied!

The Secret to a Truly Authentic Voice: Add More Context!

While the five questions above are a great starting point, the more real context you give your AI tool, the more your brand voice will sound like you—not a template.

  • Paste in a favorite outreach email, follow-up, or proposal you’ve sent.
    (“Here’s something I wrote that felt really ‘me’…”)
  • Share your mission statement, work philosophy, or a few lines from your bio or LinkedIn.
  • List phrases or words you always use with clients (or those you avoid!).
  • Describe a recent sales win or client conversation that made you proud.
  • Talk about what makes your approach different from your competitors.
  • Include direct quotes from client feedback or testimonials, if you have them.
✔️ Copied!

Why Give More Context?

The more detail you provide, the more your AI tool will “pick up” on your tone, style, and the unique ways you connect with people.
A good prompt = a good output. A great prompt = a brand voice that truly feels like you.

You can update your brand voice summary as you grow in your career or as your style evolves. Save your current “voice paragraph” and refresh it every quarter or after major wins.

Remember:
Your brand voice is your “signature.” Make it real, make it consistent—and let AI help you express it everywhere you connect.

Common Mistakes & How to Fix Them

  • Sending AI output “as-is” — The most effective use of AI is when it supports your existing style, relationships, and credibility—never as a substitute.
  • Skipping the “human in the loop” step — AI is a tool to help you express your approach and deepen relationships, not a replacement for your judgment or experience.
  • Using AI for “spray and pray” mass outreach — The best results come when AI output is customized to reflect your expertise and connection.
